About me

Hi there, my name is Michelle I LOVE flowers. I have been studying about flowers and went to Flower and Floral University in Alberta, Canada. If you have questions about flowers I am your girl. I collect rare plants and my house is just filled with lively plants that I take care of and love.

Asian Flowers

Flowers that are commonly found in Asia are Chinese Roses, Peony, Lotus, Plum Blossom, Camellia, Azalea.

European Flowers

Flowers that are found within Europe are Hydrangeas, Lavender, Cherry Blossoms, Wild Orchids, Poppies.

South American Flowers

Flowers you could find on your travels to South America could be: Orchids, Cacao, Passion Fruit Flower, Bromeliads, and Monkey Brush Vine.
